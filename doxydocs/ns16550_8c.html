NS16550 UART initialization The procedure is the following:

  • Init the baudrate through de DLAB registers
  • Configure register: 8bits word, no parity check, 1 stop bit
  • Enable interrupts
  • Disable FIFO
    Parameters
    cdevthe char device
    addressthe base NS16550 MMIO address
    baudratethe baudrate of the UART

Can't find it elsewhere but xv6 assume a 1843200hz frequency for the uart TODO: handle the PSD register
