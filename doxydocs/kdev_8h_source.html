7 \file kernel/kdev.h
8 \author Nolan Bled
9 \brief Generic device management functions
10
11 To simplify device operations, this module provides functions and structures to allocate
12 and release devices dynamically. When the platform is initialized (arch_init) it should detect
13 the hardware and allocates as much devices as it needs. The devices are stored in the kernel heap
14 and linked to each other in a global linked-list (devList). Each device has a "major" number
15 (its tag) indicating his type (tty, icu, ...) and a minor number, which is incremented everytime
16 the a new device of the same type is created. Example:
17
18 When the OS starts we have:
19 devList
20 .-------------------.
21 | .next = &devList |
22 | .prev = &devList |
23 .-------------------.
24
25 We then allocate a TTY: dev_alloc(TTY_DEV, sizeof(struct tty_s))
26 devList tty0
27 .---------------. .------------------.
28 | .next = tty0 . <--- | .next = devList; |
29 | .prev = tty0 . ---> | .prev = devList; |
30 .---------------. .------------------.
31 | .tag = TTY_DEV |
32 | .no = 0 |
33 .------------------.
34
35 Then an ICU: dev_alloc(ICU_DEV, sizeof(struct icu_s))
36 devList tty0 icu0
37 .---------------. .------------------. .-------------------.
38 | .next = tty0 . <--- | .next = icu0; | <--- | .next = devList; |
39 | .prev = icu0 . ---> | .prev = devList; | ---> | .prev = tty0; |
40 .---------------. .------------------. .-------------------.
41 | .tag = TTY_DEV | | .tag = ICU_DEV |
42 | .no = 0 | | .no = 0 |
43 .------------------. .-------------------.
44
45 And another TTY: dev_alloc(TTY_DEV, sizeof(struct tty_s))
46 devList tty0 icu0 tty1
47 .---------------. .------------------. .-------------------. .------------------.
48 | .next = tty0 . <--- | .next = icu0; | <--- | .next = tty1; | <--- | .next = devList; |
49 | .prev = icu0 . ---> | .prev = devList; | ---> | .prev = tty0; | ---> | .prev = icu0; |
50 .---------------. .------------------. .-------------------. .------------------.
51 | .tag = TTY_DEV | | .tag = ICU_DEV | | .tag = TTY_DEV |
52 | .no = 0 | | .no = 0 | | .no = 1 |
53 .------------------. .-------------------. .------------------.
54
55 Since this module manipulates only dev_s structures, each device should provide macro to
56 simplify their manipulation, by example, tty module should provide:
57 #define tty_alloc() (struct tty_s*) (dev_alloc(TTY_DEV, sizeof(struct tty_s))->data)
58 #define tty_get(no) (struct tty_s*) (dev_get(TTY_DEV, no)->data)
